On Task

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

On Task is operated by and is available at ontaskhq.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the On Task iOS app and website.

On Task is currently a US-only service. If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at privacy@ontaskhq.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address and display name. This information is required to create and manage your account.

Task and Schedule Data

We store your tasks, subtasks, sections, lists, due dates, priorities, completion status, and scheduling preferences on our servers. This data is required to power intelligent scheduling and to sync your tasks across devices.

Calendar Data

If you grant calendar access, On Task reads your calendar events to avoid scheduling conflicts and writes new time blocks when tasks are scheduled. This access uses Apple EventKit (for Apple Calendar) or Google Calendar OAuth (for Google Calendar). Calendar content is not retained beyond the active scheduling operation.

Commitment Contract Data

When you create a commitment contract, we store your chosen stake amount, selected charity, task deadline, and charge history. On Task uses Stripe to store your payment method as a SetupIntent token. On Task never receives or stores your raw card number — all payment data is handled by Stripe under PCI SAQ A scope.

Proof Media (Photo and Video)

When you submit photo or video proof for a staked task, the media is processed by our AI model to verify task completion. At submission, you are offered the option to "Attach to completed task." If you select this option, the media is retained as a completion record for the lifetime of the task. If you do not select this option, the media is permanently deleted immediately after verification.

Watch Mode Camera Data

During a Watch Mode focus session, individual video frames from your Apple Watch camera are processed in-flight by our AI model to verify that you are present and working. Frames are never stored. We retain only session metadata: start time, end time, associated task, and verification result.

HealthKit Data

If you grant HealthKit permission, On Task reads specific health metrics (such as activity data) solely to verify completion of health-related tasks. This data is not shared with any third party and is not retained beyond the verification event.

Usage Data

We collect app interaction logs, crash reports, and performance diagnostics via standard iOS crash reporting. This data is used to fix bugs and improve the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and provide the On Task service, including intelligent scheduling and task management
  • Process commitment contract charges and disburse penalties to your chosen charity
  • Verify task completion via AI-based proof review (photo, video, Watch Mode, HealthKit)
  • Sync your tasks with your calendar
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, and service updates
  • Diagnose issues and improve the performance and reliability of the app

4. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing and card tokenization. On Task uses Stripe for all financial transactions. Your payment data is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy. On Task never receives or stores raw card numbers.
  • Every.org — charity disbursement. When a commitment contract penalty is triggered, 50% of the charge amount is disbursed to your chosen charity via Every.org. Disbursement data (amount and charity name) is shared with Every.org for processing.
  • Google Calendar (optional) — if you connect Google Calendar, On Task reads and writes to your calendar via OAuth. Your Google Calendar data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. On Task does not retain calendar content beyond active scheduling operations.
  • Apple HealthKit (optional) — health data is read via HealthKit solely for proof verification of health-related tasks. This data is not shared with any third party and is not retained beyond the verification event.
  • Apple Watch (optional) — Watch Mode uses the Watch camera for passive focus session monitoring. Frame data is processed in-flight and is never stored.

5. Data Retention

  • Task and account data — retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Proof media (attached to task) — retained until the associated task is deleted
  • Proof media (not attached) — deleted immediately after AI verification
  • Watch Mode frames — never stored; only session metadata is retained
  • HealthKit data — not retained beyond the verification event
  • Payment tokens and charge records — payment method tokens are managed by Stripe. On Task retains charge records (amount, date, task, charity) for legal and tax compliance purposes.
  • After account deletion — all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

6. Your Rights

  • Access — you can view your data within the On Task app at any time.
  • Export — you can request an export of your personal data by emailing privacy@ontaskhq.com.
  • Deletion — you can delete your account via Settings → Account → Delete Account. All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Commitment contract charge records may be retained as required by law.
  • Correction — contact privacy@ontaskhq.com to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • California residents (CCPA) — you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of your data. On Task does not sell personal data.
  • GDPR (EU/UK users) — On Task is currently a US-only service. If you are in the EU or UK, please be aware that GDPR-specific protections are not yet fully implemented. Contact privacy@ontaskhq.com with any concerns.

7. Watch Mode & Camera — Special Disclosure

Watch Mode is a focus tool that uses your Apple Watch camera as a digital body double — ambient presence, not surveillance. During a Watch Mode session, the Watch camera periodically captures individual frames of your work environment. These frames are analyzed by our AI model to verify that you are present and working.

No video is recorded or stored. Individual frames are processed in-flight and discarded immediately. The only data retained is session metadata: start time, end time, the task being worked on, and the verification result.

Watch Mode is entirely opt-in. You choose it as your proof method when creating a staked task, or as a standalone focus mode without any financial commitment.

8. HealthKit — Special Disclosure

On Task requests HealthKit access only when you choose HealthKit-verified proof for a specific task, and only to verify that task's completion criteria (for example, confirming a workout was logged). HealthKit data is read once for the verification event and is not retained.

HealthKit data is never used for advertising and is never shared with third parties. On Task does not write to HealthKit.

9. Children's Privacy

On Task is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child's data has been collected, please contact us at privacy@ontaskhq.com and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, we will notify you via the app or by email.
