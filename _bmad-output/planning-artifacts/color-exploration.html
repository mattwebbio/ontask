💡 Hybrid option worth considering

Direction A's indigo base is the most distinctive and ownable in the productivity space. Direction B's gold completion moment is genuinely special — warm, earned, celebratory. These could be combined: indigo as the primary brand colour (navigation, UI chrome, committed task cards), gold reserved exclusively for completion and achievement moments (the "release" animation, the impact dashboard, stake-released confirmations). Two colours with two distinct emotional registers — commitment vs. celebration.

Indigo — commitment, structure, locking in
+
Gold — completion, release, achievement
